# Text Entries 📝

Text entries are the simplest way to add knowledge to Konvoq. Just type or paste a short piece of text — no file, no URL, no formatting required.

Manage text entries at **[Dashboard → Knowledge → Text](https://app.konvoq.com)**.

---

## What are text entries for?

Text entries are ideal for **short, factual information** that doesn't fit neatly into a document or FAQ:

- 🕐 Business hours (_"We're open Monday to Friday, 9am–6pm EST"_)
- 📍 Office address
- 💰 Pricing tiers (_"Starter: $29/month · Pro: $79/month · Enterprise: custom"_)
- 📋 Short policy summaries
- 🗒️ Notes you want the AI to always know

---

## Adding a text entry

1. Go to **Knowledge → Text**
2. Click **Add Entry**
3. Type or paste your content
4. Click **Save**

That's it — the AI can use it immediately. No scraping, no upload, no waiting.

---

## Tips for writing good text entries

**Be direct.** Write it as a statement of fact, not as a question-and-answer.

| 🟢 Good                                                         | 🔴 Avoid                                                                          |
| --------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| _"Our support team responds within 24 hours on business days."_ | _"Q: How fast do you respond? A: 24 hours."_ — use an FAQ for this format instead |
| _"We ship to the US, UK, Canada, and Australia."_               | _"Shipping info..."_ — too vague                                                  |

**Keep entries short.** Each entry should cover one topic. If your text is more than a few paragraphs, upload it as a document instead.

---

## Deleting text entries

Click the trash icon next to any entry to delete it. The AI stops using that content immediately.
