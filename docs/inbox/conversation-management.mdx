# Conversation Management 💬

**Dashboard → Inbox** is your central view of every conversation happening — or that has happened — on your widget. Think of it like an email inbox, but for chat.

---

## Conversation states

Every conversation is in one of four states:

| State | What it means |
|-------|---------------|
| **Open** | The AI is actively handling this conversation |
| **Pending** | Visitor requested a human — waiting for your team to claim it |
| **Claimed** | A team member has taken over and is chatting live |
| **Closed** | Conversation is resolved and archived |

---

## Searching and filtering

Use the search bar and filters at the top of the Inbox to find specific conversations:

- **Search** by visitor email, name, or message content
- **Filter by status** — show only Pending conversations, for example
- **Filter by date range** — see conversations from a specific period

---

## Reading a conversation

Click any conversation to open the full transcript. You can see:

- Every message the visitor sent
- Every response the AI gave
- Any messages your team sent during a handoff
- The visitor's name and email (if collected)
- The page URL where the conversation started
- Timestamps on every message

---

## Closing conversations

A conversation should be closed when the visitor's question is resolved. Close it by:

1. Opening the conversation
2. Clicking **Close Conversation**

Closed conversations stay in the Inbox — they're never deleted. You can search and review them at any time.

---

## Exporting conversations

Need to share a transcript with your team or audit a conversation? Click the conversation, then **Export → Copy transcript** to get a plain-text version.
