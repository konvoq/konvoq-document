# Usage & Limits 📊

Every Konvoq plan includes limits on how much you can use certain features. This page explains what's tracked and what happens when you get close to a limit.

Go to **[Dashboard → Usage](https://app.konvoq.com)** to see your current numbers.

---

## What's tracked

| Metric             | What it counts                                  |
| ------------------ | ----------------------------------------------- |
| **Conversations**  | Total conversations started this billing period |
| **Vector storage** | Number of knowledge chunks stored in your index |
| **Scraped pages**  | Website pages currently indexed                 |
| **Team seats**     | Active team members in your workspace           |
| **Active flows**   | Number of live conversation flows               |

---

## Understanding vector storage

When you add content to your knowledge base (documents, FAQs, scraped pages), Konvoq converts each piece into a **vector** — a mathematical representation that makes semantic search possible.

Each chunk of content uses one vector slot. A typical document might produce 20–50 vectors. A large website scrape might produce several thousand.

Your plan includes a vector storage limit. When you're near the limit:

- The usage page shows a warning banner
- The AI keeps working until you hit the hard limit
- You can free up space by deleting outdated scraped pages or documents

---

## What happens at the limit?

| Metric         | At the limit                                                |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| Conversations  | New conversations are queued or blocked (depending on plan) |
| Vector storage | New content can't be indexed until space is freed           |
| Scraped pages  | New scrape jobs are blocked                                 |
| Team seats     | New invitations are blocked                                 |
| Active flows   | No new flows can be activated                               |

---

## Upgrading your plan

Go to **Account → Billing** to see available plans and upgrade. Changes take effect immediately — new limits apply as soon as payment is confirmed.

:::tip Delete before you upgrade
Before upgrading just to get more vector storage, check if you have old scraped pages from before a major redesign. Deleting stale content is free and often frees up significant space.
:::
