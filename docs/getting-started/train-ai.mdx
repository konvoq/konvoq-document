# Train the AI 🧠

Konvoq doesn't know anything about your product out of the box. You need to **teach it** — like handing a new employee your company handbook and making them read the whole thing.

The more content you add, the smarter and more accurate the answers get.

---

## Where to manage content

Everything lives in **[Dashboard → Knowledge](https://app.konvoq.com)**. You'll see four types of content you can add:

| Source           | Best for                                |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| 🌐 Scraped pages | Your website, help center, blog         |
| 📄 Documents     | PDFs, Word docs, spreadsheets           |
| ❓ FAQs          | Exact Q&A pairs with definitive answers |
| 📝 Text entries  | Short facts — hours, pricing, addresses |

---

## Scrape your website

This is the fastest way to get started. Konvoq visits your website pages automatically and reads them, just like a search engine crawler.

1. Go to **Knowledge → Scrape URLs**
2. Enter your website's URL (e.g. `https://yoursite.com`)
3. Click **Start Scraping**
4. Watch the status update in real time — when pages show `done`, the AI is already using them

:::tip Start with your most important pages
Point Konvoq at your help center, product pages, and pricing page first. That's what visitors ask about most.
:::

---

## Upload documents

Got a PDF manual, a Word doc with your policies, or a spreadsheet of product specs? Upload them directly.

**Supported formats:** PDF, DOCX, TXT, CSV, XLSX, XLS
**Max file size:** 10 MB per file

1. Go to **Knowledge → Documents**
2. Click **Upload**
3. Select your file — Konvoq extracts the text, splits it into small chunks, and makes it searchable

---

## Add FAQs

FAQs are **exact-match answers** — when a visitor asks something that closely matches your FAQ question, Konvoq returns that answer directly (before even searching your other content).

Use them for questions where there's one correct answer:

> _"What is your refund policy?"_
> _"What hours is support available?"_
> _"How much does the Pro plan cost?"_

1. Go to **Knowledge → FAQs**
2. Click **Add FAQ**
3. Type the question _as a visitor would ask it_
4. Type the complete answer
5. Save

---

## Add text entries

Text entries are small, freeform knowledge snippets. Perfect for information that doesn't fit neatly into a document or FAQ:

- Business hours
- Office address
- Short policy summaries
- Pricing tiers

Go to **Knowledge → Text → Add Entry**, paste your content, and save. It's available to the AI immediately.

---

## Check what's indexed

Go to **Knowledge → Scraped Pages** to see every page Konvoq has read, its current status, and how many pieces of content it produced.

| Status     | Meaning                                 |
| ---------- | --------------------------------------- |
| `done`     | ✅ Indexed and ready                    |
| `scraping` | 🔄 Still crawling                       |
| `indexing` | 🔄 Processing content                   |
| `failed`   | ❌ Something went wrong — check the URL |

---

## Keep it fresh

The AI only knows what you've given it. If you publish new pages or update your docs, re-scrape or re-upload so the AI gets the latest version.

:::warning Stale content = wrong answers
If you update your pricing page but don't re-scrape it, Konvoq will still quote the old price. Make it a habit to re-scrape after major content updates.
:::
