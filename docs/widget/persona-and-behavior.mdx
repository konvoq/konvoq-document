# Persona & Behavior 🗣️

Your AI assistant doesn't have to feel robotic. Give it a name, a friendly greeting, and a tone that sounds like your brand. Visitors will feel like they're talking to a real team member — just one that never sleeps.

All persona settings live at **[Dashboard → Widget → Persona](https://app.konvoq.com)**.

---

## Assistant name

This is the name shown in the chat header and in each message the assistant sends.

**Default:** `AI Assistant`

Set it to something that fits your brand:

- `Aria` — friendly and approachable
- `Support Bot` — clear and functional
- `Max` — conversational and casual

Keep it short. One word is usually best.

---

## Welcome message

The welcome message is the **very first thing** a visitor sees when they open the chat. You have one shot to make them feel welcome and tell them what to do.

**Good examples:**

> _"Hi! 👋 How can I help you today?"_
> _"Hey there! Ask me anything about our product."_
> _"Welcome! I'm Aria — I can answer questions about pricing, setup, and more."_

**Not so good:**

> _"Hello. I am an automated assistant. Please type your query."_ ❌

Keep it warm, short, and actionable.

---

## AI system prompt

The system prompt is like an **instruction letter** you give the AI before it talks to anyone. It controls:

- **Tone** — Should it be professional? Casual? Technical?
- **Scope** — Should it only answer questions about your product, or can it discuss general topics?
- **Language** — By default, Konvoq matches the visitor's language automatically
- **Lead capture** — When should it ask for the visitor's contact info?

Go to **Dashboard → AI Prompts** to write and manage system prompts.

### Auto-generate a prompt

Not sure what to write? Click **Generate Prompt** — Konvoq analyzes your scraped content and suggests a system prompt based on your brand and what your site is about. You can edit it before activating.

## Widget purpose templates

Pick a pre-configured purpose to get sensible defaults without writing a prompt from scratch:

| Template              | What it optimizes for                           |
| --------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| **Customer Support**  | Answering questions, reducing support tickets   |
| **Sales Assistant**   | Qualifying leads, answering pricing questions   |
| **Internal Helpdesk** | Answering employee questions from internal docs |

You can still customize the auto-generated prompt after choosing a template.
