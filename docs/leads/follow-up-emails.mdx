# Follow-Up Emails ✉️

After a visitor leaves a conversation, Konvoq can automatically send them a follow-up email — a friendly nudge that keeps your business top of mind.

Set this up at **[Dashboard → Leads → Follow-Up](https://app.konvoq.com)**.

---

## Why follow-up emails matter

Most visitors who chat with your widget won't convert on their first visit. A well-timed follow-up email — sent automatically, with no effort from your team — gives them a reason to come back.

---

## Enabling follow-up emails

1. Go to **Leads → Follow-Up**
2. Toggle **Auto Follow-Up** on
3. Set the **delay** — how long after the conversation ends before the email is sent
   - 30 minutes works well for most use cases
   - Use a longer delay (e.g. 2 hours) for technical support conversations
4. Customize the **email subject** and **body**
5. Save

---

## Template variables

Personalize the email with variables that Konvoq fills in automatically:

| Variable    | What it inserts                   |
| ----------- | --------------------------------- |
| `{{name}}`  | The visitor's name (if collected) |
| `{{email}}` | The visitor's email address       |

**Example subject:** `Hi {{name}}, here's a recap from our chat`

**Example body:**

```
Hi {{name}},

Thanks for chatting with us today! If you have any more questions,
just reply to this email — we're happy to help.

The team at [Your Company]
```

---

:::tip Keep follow-up emails short
A 3–4 line email that feels personal gets far better responses than a long marketing email. The goal is to open a door, not close a sale.
:::
