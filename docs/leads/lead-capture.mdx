# Lead Capture 📋

Konvoq can ask visitors for their contact information during a conversation and save them as leads — automatically. No forms on a separate page, no interruptions, just a natural ask at the right moment.

Configure this at **[Dashboard → Leads → Settings](https://app.konvoq.com)**.

---

## How it works

The AI watches for the right moment during a conversation, then naturally asks:

> _"Before I continue — can I get your name and email so we can follow up if needed?"_

When the visitor provides their details, Konvoq saves them as a lead in your dashboard.

---

## Enabling lead capture

1. Go to **Dashboard → Leads → Settings**
2. Toggle **Lead Capture** on
3. Choose **when to prompt** the visitor:

| Timing option       | Best for                          |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| After first message | High-intent pages (pricing, demo) |
| After N messages    | When the visitor is engaged       |
| On specific intent  | Pricing questions, demo requests  |

---

## Viewing your leads

Go to **Dashboard → Leads** to see every captured contact:

| Column           | What it shows                      |
| ---------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Name & email     | The contact information provided   |
| Source URL       | Which page they were on            |
| Timestamp        | When the lead was captured         |
| Follow-up status | Whether a follow-up email was sent |

---

## Sending leads to your CRM

Use **webhooks** to automatically push new leads to HubSpot, your own backend, or any other CRM. See [Webhooks](../integrations/webhooks) and [HubSpot Integration](../integrations/hubspot) for details.

---

## Exporting leads

Download all leads as a CSV from **Leads → Export**. The file includes name, email, source URL, and timestamp.
