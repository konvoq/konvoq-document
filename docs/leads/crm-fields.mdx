# CRM Fields 🗂️

CRM Fields control what information Konvoq collects when it captures a lead during a conversation.

Configure these at **[Dashboard → Leads → CRM Settings](https://app.konvoq.com)**.

---

## Default fields

Out of the box, Konvoq collects:

| Field     | Type              | Required |
| --------- | ----------------- | -------- |
| **Name**  | Text              | Optional |
| **Email** | Email (validated) | Required |

The email field is always required — it's the primary identifier for a lead.

---

## How fields are collected

Konvoq asks for information conversationally — not as a rigid form. The AI works the fields into the conversation naturally:

> _"What's your name?"_
> _"And what's the best email to reach you at?"_

This feels much less intrusive than stopping the conversation to fill out a form.

---

## Exporting leads with field data

All collected field data is included in the CSV export from **Leads → Export**. Each column corresponds to a field — making it easy to import into any CRM or spreadsheet.

---

:::info Connecting to HubSpot
If you use HubSpot, you can map Konvoq fields directly to HubSpot contact properties. See the [HubSpot Integration](../integrations/hubspot) page for setup.
:::
