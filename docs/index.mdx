# Welcome to Konvoq Docs 👋

Think of Konvoq as a **really smart helper** that lives on your website. It reads all your docs, FAQs, and web pages — then answers customer questions automatically, 24/7, without anyone on your team having to lift a finger.

## Get started in 3 steps

| Step  | What you do                                            | What happens                        |
| ----- | ------------------------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------- |
| **1** | [Install the widget](./getting-started/install-widget) | Konvoq appears on your site         |
| **2** | [Train the AI](./getting-started/train-ai)             | It learns from your content         |
| **3** | [Go live](./getting-started/go-live-checklist)         | Real visitors start getting answers |
